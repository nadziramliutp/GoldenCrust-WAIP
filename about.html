Who we are & What we do?

At GoldenCrust, we are passionate about bringing the art of pastry-making to life with every bite. Specializing in a wide variety of freshly baked pastries, from delicate croissants, macaron and rich tarts to decadent cakes and savory bites, our mission is to satisfy cravings and create unforgettable experiences for our customers through online services for more convenient lifestyle.

GoldenCrust will provide an affordable price for all of our customers to ensure everyone have a chance to taste these delicates especially for students to share and enjoy with their friends while studying.
